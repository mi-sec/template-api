bytes

The byte is a unit of digital information that most commonly consists of eight bits. Pertains to this application as a number or string expected to be a digital information measurement of "bytes"

Recognized conversion units:

(Unit)[Standard Base 10][Digital Storage Unit 2ⁿ]
(B)BytesBytes
(kB|KiB)KilobytesKibibytes
(mB|MiB)MegabytesMegibytes
(gB|GiB)GigabytesGigibytes
(tB|TiB)TerabytesTeribytes
